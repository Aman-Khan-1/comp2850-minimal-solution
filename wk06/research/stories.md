# Job Stories - Week 6 Needs-Finding

## Story S1: Balancing tasks
**Situation**: When I am balancing attending a swimming class with my emails.
**Motivation**: I want to get reminders for all of my tasks
**Outcome**: So I can keep on track on what exactly I have to do and when. 
**Underlying need**: Because remembering about all of these different situational tasks (physical/mental) is exhausting for my brain.

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q1 L12)
**Inclusion risk**: Cognitive, ADHD
**Type**: Job story (situation-specific)

---

## Story S2: App navigation
**Situation**: Navigating through the app that has menu icons and/or other icons.
**Motivation**: I need the layout to be simple and have the text size reasonably large.
**Outcome**: This allows easy reading and understanding of the layout of the page.
**Underlying need**: Because small text size and icons make it very difficult to read and even use the app without it's understanding. 

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q2 L24)
**Inclusion risk**: Colour imparement, visual impairment.
**Type**: Job story

---

## Story 3: Attending parent's evening
**Situation**: Attending a parent-teacher meeting.
**Motivation**: I need a reminder before the meeting starts.
**Outcome**: So that I do not forget about it.
**Underlying need** Because my memory is not the best, especially if I am juggling lots of tasks daily.

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q3 L36)
**Inclusion risk**: Screen readers, hearing impairments
**Type**: Job story


---

## Story 4: Partially-sighted adapting to their workflow
**Situation**: Navigating through apps/websites without a mouse
**Motivation**: I rely on keyboard shortcuts or voice commands
**Outcome**: Navigating through the apps/websites easily
**Underlying need**: Easier and faster than trying to track the cursor all the time

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q4 L48)
**Inclusion risk**: Dyslexic, speech-impaired
**Type**: Job story
**WCAG**: 2.1.1 Keyboard (A)

## Story 5: High contrast 
**Situation**: Working outside in bright sunlight
**Motivation**: I want to be able to see the text, against high contrast.
**Outcome**: So I can read app buttons and task titles without straining.
**Underlying need**: Because low contrast creates situational disability (low vision)

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q5 L60)
**Inclusion risk**: Low vision, colour-blindness
**Type**: Job story
**WCAG**: 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum, AA) - 4:5:1 for normal text

## Story 6: Task prioritisation
**Situation**: Prioritising what tasks to do
**Motivation**: The app suggests tasks to focus on based on importance or deadlines
**Outcome**: Which can help me save energy and concentrate on what matters most.
**Underlying need**: Because figuring out which task is more important is tiring.

**Evidence**: Participant A (notes Q6 L72)
**Inclusion risk**: Cognitive, digital literacty
**Type**: Job story
